NSE/BSE

NSE:: The National Stock Exchange of India Limited (NSE), headquartered in Mumbai, is the country's premier financial marketplace. Established in 1992, it has grown into a highly sophisticated, fully automated electronic trading platform, providing seamless access to investors nationwide.
BSE: BSE Limited, also known as the Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE), is an Indian stock exchange based in Mumbai. Established in 1875, it is the oldest stock exchange in Asia, and also the tenth oldest in the world. It is the 6th largest stock exchange in the world by total market capitalization, exceeding $5 trillion in May 2024.
